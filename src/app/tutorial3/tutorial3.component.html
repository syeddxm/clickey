EARDU SCANNING TUTORIAL

Now you will be walked through 4 different keyboard and scanning methods

First, you will practice using the scanning method on the QWERTY keyboard

1. Press either Shift key to begin

2. The keyboard will begin scanning downwards

3. When highlighted row contains desired key, press either shift again

4. The keyboard will begin scanning from left to right

5. When desired is highlighted, press either shift again

6. Press either shift key to begin again

There is no way to backspace, just continue to writing the next key
